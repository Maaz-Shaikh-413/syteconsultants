WHY AGENT REGISTRATION MATTERS

Legal Requirement

RERA mandates that all real estate agents must be registered to operate legally. Non-compliance can result in severe penalties.

Consumer Protection

Agent registration under RERA is aimed at safeguarding the interests of homebuyers and sellers. It ensures transparency and accountability in real estate transactions.

Enhanced Credibility

RERA registration enhances your credibility as a real estate professional, instilling confidence in clients and business partners.

Access to Information

Registered agents gain access to a wealth of information and data about registered real estate projects, which can be valuable for business growth.
